My hobbies

My hobbies are:

  • Exploring science and tech in general
  • Working with voice recognition systems and robots
  • Dreaming big about creating solutions for real-world problems
  • Learning about new tech like TensorFlow and object detection
  • Playing around with hardware, like motors and sensors, for cool projects


I have a lot of hobbies that keep me creative, curious, and excited every day. One of my favorite hobbies is working with Arduino and electronics. I love building cool projects like robots, smart systems, and sensors. Coding in Python is another hobby I enjoy—I’ve used it for face detection, object tracking, and even AI models. I also love flying and building planes and drones. Designing PCBs and circuits is super fun for me, especially when everything works perfectly. I enjoy experimenting with new sensors and modules that come in my Arduino kit. Learning about fighter jets and missile systems is something I find really interesting. I spend time creating projects that solve real-world problems like smart roads and clean water. Another hobby of mine is web development—I’ve built my own personal website with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I like drawing rough sketches of my ideas before turning them into real tech. Watching science videos and learning new technologies keeps me inspired. I also enjoy reading about scientists and how they changed the world. Sometimes I just sit with a notebook and brainstorm futuristic inventions. I love exploring AI tools like TensorFlow and how they can help in real life. I’m always trying to learn something new each day. Tinkering and debugging is fun for me—not stressful. I enjoy participating in science fairs and competitions like WRO. Making my own projects and showing them to others makes me proud. In short, my hobbies are all about learning, building, and dreaming big!
